To invoke a test from the command line:

$ .\vendor\bin\phpunit --bootstrap .\vendor\autoload.php --verbose .\tests\LoanOfficerDelegateTest.php --colors=always

OR "w/ --testdox"

$ .\vendor\bin\phpunit --bootstrap .\vendor\autoload.php --testdox tests
